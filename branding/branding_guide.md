## Color Palette

| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deep Navy** | `#001F3F` | Primary background, headers, and text. |
| **Electric Blue** | `#0074D9` | Call to actions, accents, and icons. |
| **Slate White** | `#F4F7F6` | Secondary backgrounds and subtle dividers. |
| **Accent Cyan** | `#39CCCC` | Hover states and highlights. |

---

## Typography Recommendation
- **Primary Font**: `Inter` or `Roboto` (Sans-serif) - for a clean, modern look.
- **Heading Font**: `Outfit` or `Montserrat` - for a professional and bold presence.

---

## Implementation Rules
1. Always maintain clear space around the logo.
2. Do not distort or change the logo colors.
3. Use **Deep Navy** for dark mode backgrounds and **Slate White** for light mode surfaces.
